파

You’ll find that ㅍ looks a bit like a compressed ㅂ (I wonder why). ㅍ corresponds with the English letter “P” and you say ㅍ perfectly when you use the English word “power”. This poppy, aspirated ”P” is exactly what we’re going for with the ㅍ pronunciation. Got it right?

ㅍ is somewhat of an exception to the rule of adding a line to make an aspirated consonant. When you compare it to ㅂ however, you could say it looks like they added one long line that sticks out of the bottom on both sides then they smushed the top lines of ㅂ down flat.
