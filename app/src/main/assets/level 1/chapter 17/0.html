Chapter 17

Marking Direction with 에

Man I’m hungry, aren’t you? I hope you enjoyed that taste of Korean cuisine. Now it’s time to feed your intellect again.

If we were to make a list of the most fundamental and crucial particles in Korean, 에 would definitely be in the top five on the list. This is because 에 wields the power to define targets, pinpoint times, specify locations and point out directions. If there were no particle to fill this function you would never be able to answer the questions “where” or “when”. 에 is going to become one of our well used and trusty tools as we progress through learning Korean.
