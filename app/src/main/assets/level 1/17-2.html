“저녁은 7시에 먹어요?”

In this sentence 에 is used to mark the time that something will happen. In English we usually use “at” when pointing out specific times, though in some cases we use “on” as in “on Thursday”. There's none of this silliness in Korean. 에 is used to mark all points in time.

목요일에
10월에
다음주에
저번에
작년에

As you can see 에 is extremely flexible and useful when it comes to marking the time.
