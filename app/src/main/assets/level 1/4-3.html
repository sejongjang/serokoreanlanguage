So what about groups outside of BTS? Well we emphasized BTS because they have found the most success of any group so far, but that's not to say there aren't other hugely successful and popular groups. Quite the opposite in fact. All of these groups (and solo artists) are definitely worth checking out and there are many more talented artists beside these! There’s also an unusual phenomenon that’s almost unique to the Korean entertainment industry. The versatility of these stars allows them to do everything. It’s not unusual for a celebrity to be involved in a band, film, drama, lend their voice in creation of the soundtrack for the drama, appear in product-endorsement commercials, participate in comedy shows, and user for fashion shows and magazines all at the same time! While many western celebrities are typically limited to just acting, or just singing, you’ll be astonished to see your favorite star doing everything and appearing everywhere on TV and the internet in Korea. And the really crazy thing is that many of these stars succeed in doing it all. Just look at IU (also listed above) who is a hugely popular singer, song-writer, model, and actress domestically in Korea. In fact she's know there as "Korea's little sister".
