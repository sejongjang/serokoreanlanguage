For our first little segment I’ve elected to talk about one of the main reasons this app exists and the reason many people become interested in learning Korean. That reason is 한류(韓流), known in English as “The Korean Wave”.

What is the Korean Wave? Put simply, it’s a revolution in the entertainment industry that is taking the world by storm. Starting about ten years ago (with roots going back much further), Korean movies, music, and television began gaining serious international recognition. Since then the Korean Wave has only gotten bigger and gained more momentum.

In a world dominated by Hollywood, many people have found Korean storytelling techniques to be refreshing. The stories are character-driven and uplifting. Korean directors rely less on splashy special effects and more on impressively talented and good-looking actors and actresses, coupled with hearty writing. If you’ve never watched a Korean drama I highly recommend trying one out. I’ll just warn you that they can be extremely addictive!
