Before wrapping up this chapter there are a few "special cases", or unexpected sounds, we need to talk about. Mostly these have to do with carry-over sounds. We've actually alaredy talked about some of these. Those are shown below.

  • Despite making other sounds on top, the letters make a t-stop sound in the 받침 position are ㄷ, ㅅ, ㅈ, ㅊ, ㅌ, and also ㅆ.
  • ㄴ-ㄹ carry-over sounds like a ㄹ-ㄹ carry-over.
  • ㅎ has the special ability to make non-aspirated consonants sound aspirated.

These are some of the unexpected behaviors we've gone over but there are a few more we need let let you know about. We'll start with the m-stop sound.
