ㅃ can be difficult to distinguish from ㅂ and ㅍ despite it following the regular pattern for double consonants. Listen very carefully to these words below.

아바

아파

아빠

오바

오파

오빠

반대

판대

빤대

In the first column, we can hear that ㅂ is like the plain English “B” sound, but the second two are trickier. With ㅍ you give that little burst of air and flex of the stomach (like the English letter P). But with ㅃ your mouth starts small (tensed) then it suddenly opens wide to give it a strong “B”. Making sure you get this down now will help your speaking and listening later.
