For our first little culture segment let's talk about one of the main reasons this app exists and the reason many people become interested in learning Korean. That reason is 한류(韓流), otherwise known in English as the Korean Wave.

What is the Korean Wave? Put simply, it’s everything Korean that is taking the world by storm. Starting about twenty years ago (with roots going back even further), Korean products, food, and most notably movies, music, and television began gaining serious international recognition. Since then the Korean Wave has only gotten bigger and gained more momentum.

You must have noticed some of those big name brands in the vocabulary list but you might not have know they were Korean! Now you know the proper way to pronounce these company names when you go to buy a new phone or car.







Famous korean foods such as bulgogi, bibimbap, and korean barbeque buffets are also gaining extreme popularity in the America's and Europe. We won't go into detail on food here because we're going to hit it hard in chapter 16 but just keep in mind that food is also one aspect of 한류.

The most notable aspect of this wave of Korean culture may be Korean music and dramas. In a world dominated by Hollywood and western culture, many people have found Korean entertaiment to be refreshing and new. K-pop music videos are colorful, shiny, and enthralling. The stories in Korean dramas are character-driven and uplifting. Korean directors rely less on splashy special effects and more on impressively talented and good-looking actors and actresses, coupled with hearty writing. If you’ve never watched a Korean drama the time has come. We'll just warn you that they can be extremely addictive!
