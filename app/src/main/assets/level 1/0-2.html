Rather than dividing the program into beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels, Sero Korean is divided into numbered levels. This approach gives us the flexibility to continue to add valuable content at a reasonable pace. Each level countains 30 chapters, and the chapters are not long. In fact, each chapter is designed to be completed within 20-30 minutes. This enables anyone to find time to do at least one chapter per day and complete an entire level in just one month.

Level one totals in at over 50,000 words, with 300+ examples sentences (including native-speaker recordings), 500+ vocabulary words, and much more. You should never feel like you’re drowning, since we’ve worked hard to strike the right balance between pushing forward quickly and taking enough time to understand concepts thoroughly.

Now is also a good time to mention that there are quizzes at the end of most chapters. (There are a few culture chapters where we just want to give you a break). Some chapters will have grammar quizzes, some will have vocabulary quizzes, and some will have both. Don't sweat these too much because you can always retake them. Additionally, once you take the quiz on a vocabulary word it will be added to your 'In-progress' list, which you can find in the 'Vocab' section of the app.

Some sections also feature popouts which contain notes from the authors. If you are just trying to finish as fast as you can feel free to skip over these pop-outs as they contain no vital information. Tap on the arrow here to see what they look like.
