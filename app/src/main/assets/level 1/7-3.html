Let's look at some moreFragment example dialogue with our characters now.

재영씨 차가 있어요?

Jaeyoung, do you have a car?

(tap for translation)


아니요, 차 없어요. 애슐리씨 있어요?

No I don't have one. Do you?

(tap for translation)


아니요.

No.

(tap for translation)


By now you'll have noticed that we haven't taught you the Korean word for "you" yet. The reason is that in Korean there's not really a polite form of the word "you". What is polite is referring to people by their name and title.
