Chapter 8

A Little Place Called Korea

Chapter Insight

70 million people. 2 trillion-dollar economy. 10,000-year history. Groundbreaking technological development. All of this in a land area just over half the size of California. And these are just a small highlight of some of the impressive facts about Korea.

In this section we’ll take a little break, and go over some main points of history and achievements of Korea. I’m using the term Korea loosely to refer to an ethnic and linguistic group that dates back thousands of years and has primarily occupied the Korean peninsula. Today what we know of that group is split between “The Republic of Korea” (South Korea) and the ironically named “Democratic People’s Republic of Korea” (North Korea).

We won’t go into what caused the fracturing of the Korean peninsula here, however you may be interested to know that this isn’t the first time Korea has been split into multiple countries. Intrigued? For simplicity’s sake, we will mainly focus on Korea from around 60 BCE until the present day for this is the time period that has molded Korean culture the most into what we see today.
