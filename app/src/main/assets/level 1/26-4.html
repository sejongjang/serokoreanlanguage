Now let’s look at some example sentences that might be helpful when describing the weather.

Summer Weather

비가 와요 = It’s raining
우산을 안 가져왔어요 = I didn’t bring an umbrella
천둥 치는 소리를 들었어요 = I heard (the sound of) thunder
날씨가 너무 더워요 = The weather is too hot
바다에서 수영하고 싶어요 = I want to go swimming in the ocean
바닷가에 가고 싶어요 = I want to go to the beach
한국에서는 장마가 7월에 있어요 = In Korea, the rainy season is in July.
날씨가 좋아요 = weather is nice
에어컨을 틀어 줘요 = please turn the air conditioner on
더운 날씨가 괜찮은데 습한 날씨가 싫어요 = Hot weather is okay, but I don’t like humid weather


Fall Weather

날씨가 쌀쌀해요 = the weather is chilly
하늘이 매우 맑아요 = The sky is very clear
다음 주에 태풍이 올 거예요 = There will be a typhoon next week


Winter Weather

날씨가 추워요 = The weather is cold
눈이 와요 = It’s snowing
눈이 많이 싸였어요 = A lot of snow piled up
얼음 때문에 길이 미끄러워요 = The road is slippery because of the ice
눈을 치워야 돼요 = We have to clear the snow
추운 날씨를 좋아해요 = I like cold weather


Spring Weather

꽃이 피고 있어요 = The flowers are blooming
눈이 다 녹았어요 = The snow is all melted
날씨가 따뜻해지고 있어요 = The weather is getting warmer


General

바람이 심하게 불어요 = The wind is blowing hard
날씨는 주말에 추워졌어 = The weather got cold over the weekend.
