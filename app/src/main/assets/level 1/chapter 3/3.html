Our next vowel is 우/유.

우

유

This one is remarkably similar to the "ooh" sound in English you make when you get a painful yet refreshing massage. “Ooh, it hurts so good”. You also make this sound in words like “roof”, and “oodles”. Just keep this one nice and round and forward (like it’s buddy 오) and you’ll have it perfect.

Also, just as 오 harmonizes with 아, 우 has a harmonizing partner, 어. Together they produce 워.

워
