REVIEW

  • Use native-Korean numbers to count to 10 (or up to around 40). Go higher and you’re likely to hear people switch to the Chinese-based numbers.
  • Ordinal numbers tell us what order things happened in.
  • To make ordinal numbers add the postfix 째 onto the normal numbers (remembering 하나 become 첫).
  • Cardinal numbers are used to count things.
  • Say how old you are with cardinal numbers and the years-lived counter, 살.
  • Say what hour it is with cardinal numbers and the hour counter, 시
  • Count things in general with cardinal numbers and the counter for things, 개.
