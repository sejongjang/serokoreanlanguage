Too cold: Formal and polite speech— characterized primarily by 습니다 and 습니까 endings. This is the cold, stiff, and formal “high form”. You got a taste of this formal ending when we talked about common greetings in Chapter 6. We won’t bother going into much detail with this one at the moment because we’ll be going in depth soon. Just know that high form is used in formal situations. It’s also categorized as polite speech, which is known as 존댓말. 반말 is the opposite of 존댓말, as it is not polite.
