사라는 예쁜 여자예요.

Lit. “(as for) Sarah pretty girl is.”

In the first sentence here, we can see that 사라 is the topic, and in this case 예쁘다 has been conjugation to a noun-modifier form. Think back to chapter 13 with me now and recall that we learned the future tense modifier ㄹ/을. You can attach this modifier to verbs (and yes, adjectives too) to say that something will do or be a certain way.

밥 먹을거에요.

In this case the verb 먹다 has been conjugated to future tense form.
