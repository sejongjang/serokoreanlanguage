오랜만이에요

(tap for translation)

This is a phrase meaning “It’s been a while!”. As you’d expect it’s used when you haven’t seen someone you are close with in a long time. 오래 is a word meaning “a long time” and works as a noun or an adverb. 오랜 and 오랫 are alternative versions of the same word that are used when 오래 is combined with other words. 만 in this case means “following a period” or “after a time”. It can also be used after specific time measurements like “4년만에” which means “after four years”.
