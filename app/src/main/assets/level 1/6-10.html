Great work and before you take the quiz make sure and review these bullet points.

  • Every Korean sentence must have a verb.
  • Verbs must come at the end of clauses/sentences.
  • With a noun that has a 받침 use 이에요.
  • With a noun that doesn’t have a 받침 use 예요.
  • 아니에요 is a verb meaning "is not" and should not be confused with 아니요 meaning "no".
  • 네 can be used to express disbelief or exclaim surprise like the English word "what".
  • 이다 can express equivalency, but cannot be used to express existence.
