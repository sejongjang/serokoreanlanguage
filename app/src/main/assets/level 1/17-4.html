“개가 테이블 위에 뛰어올랐어요.”

Finally we can see that 에 is used to mark the direction of movement. There is another particle we’ll be learning that marks direction in general, but just remember that 에 is used for specific pinpointing and directions. And get used to seeing it because it is used a lot.
